* Preface
Having recently come across numerous 'literate' initialisation files using Org's
tangling features, I have decided to transition to a single monolithic
=init.org=, out of which =org-babel-tangle-file= will coax the various
initialisation files.

If nothing else, this allows me to share any little gems of Emacs which I (and
others before me) have found upon their travels. In such spirit, I strongly
advise you to visit the following (much more mature!) literate configurations.

- https://github.com/wasamasa/dotemacs/blob/master/init.org
- http://writequit.org/org/
- http://doc.rix.si/org/fsem.html
- https://postmomentum.ch/steckemacs.html
- http://pages.sachachua.com/.emacs.d/Sacha.html
- https://github.com/larstvei/dot-emacs/blob/master/init.org
